WIR-Citation-Generator

Usage of Generator

  1. Export publications from bibsonomy in BibTex Format
  2. Copy the raw bibtex Files from bibsonomy
  3. Paste in textarea
  4. Put in the year from which the citations should be used until now
  5. Citation will be generated and can be copied
                        @ARTICLE{Cesar2013,
                            author = {Jean Paul},
                            title = {An amazing not so usual title},
                            year = {2013},
                            volume = {42},
                            pages = {21--42},
                            journal = {Amazing Journal}
                        },
                        @ARTICLE{Gunther2009,
                            author = {Gunther Paulmanns},
                            title = {A wonderful title},
                            year = {2009},
                            volume = {11},
                            journal = {Title of Journal}
                        }
