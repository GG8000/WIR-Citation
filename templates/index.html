WIR-Citation-Generator

Usage

  • Export .bibTex (e. g. from bibsonomy)
  • Copy from bibsonomy the raw bibtexFile
  • Citation will be generated and can be copied
                    @ARTICLE{Cesar2013,
                        author = {Jean Paul},
                            title = {An amazing not so usual title},
                            year = {2011},
                            volume = {42},
                            pages = {21--42},
                            journal = {Amazing Journal}
                        }
